Doctor Assist AI

This prototype is being developed to make doctor-patient interactions more seamless. Now, doctors can focus on patients rather than worrying about the extensive documentations and procedures that they have to follow post consultation. The doctors can record conversations with the patients and generate transcript with speaker diarisation, SOAP format medical summary notes, follow-up questions and ICD (International Classification of Diseases) codes - All in a matter of few seconds and click of a button!

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment analyses of Yelp reviews of our favourite dinner spot - Outback Steakhouse! Here different models are used to understand how sentiment analysis can be done using BERT model. It's funny how subjective online reviews can be! We always find ourselves wondering why Outback, with its pretty darn good food, doesn't have higher ratings. The reviews were scraped from the web and then analyzed to see top reviews and most common complains from customers.

California Bay Area Auto Crime Analysis

After an unfortunate incident wherein the quarter window of our brand new car was smashed into bits during an attempted robbery (and during a global inventory shortage!!), I decided to analyze auto crimes over the past 90 days in the main cities of the Bay Area. The data was obtained from various police departments' open data initiatives. The longitude and latitude coordinates were generated from the recorded addresses using Google Maps API in R. This can now help us to identify problematic areas that we should think twice before parking!

Key components: Data Cleaning and Analysis with R, Google Maps API, Tableau
